OFS RESOURCES



The Secular Franciscan Order consists of ordinary men and women who have dedicated themselves to living the vision and spirit of St. Francis of Assisi in their daily lives. They do not take religious vows, but do make promises as members of the Secular Franciscan Order. They do not function as religious clerics but their life and ministry is in and of the world. Franciscan friars accompany them on their journey, offering advice and guidance as they seek to follow the way of St. Francis. Learn more...
